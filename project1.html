Emergency Ventilator Project

Project Details

The Emergency Ventilator is a lifesaving device designed to assist patients who are unable to breathe adequately on their own. This project focuses on developing a cost-effective, portable, and easy-to-use ventilator system that can be deployed in emergency situations, such as during natural disasters or pandemics.

Key Features:

  • Automated Breathing Control
  • Portable and Lightweight Design
  • User-Friendly Interface
  • Cost-Effective Solution for Emergencies

Specifications:

  • Power Supply: 12V Battery
  • Flow Rate: Adjustable up to 30 L/min
  • Display: LCD Screen for User Interface
